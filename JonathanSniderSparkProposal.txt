Ghost story/paranormal activity site meant to entertain whether you believe in the paranormal
or not.

uses:
1.create an account to create a horror story or share a real one you had
2.rate the spookiness of user's real experiences and the quality of horror stories.
3.find a map of popular/notable paranormal spots in your area
4. get tips for searching for and recording your own paranormal footage
5.connect to other paranormal websites in your area
6.read some of the most popular "hauntings" in history
7.get definitions for what seperates different kinds of paranormal activites (such
as the difference or lack thereof between a ghost or spirit).
