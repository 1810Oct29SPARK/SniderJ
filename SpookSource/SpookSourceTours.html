Ghost Tours in Tampa!

If you'd rather leave the ghost hunting to the people that know what they're doing, we don't blame you! Any city with a decent history is going to have a few disgruntled past residents, and Tampa is no exception. Here's a list of paranormal tours you can take in the Tampa area. Click on any image for the tour to be taken to their own website.

The Official Ybor City Ghost Tour

Ybor City is a district of Tampa located Northeast of Downtown that was founded in 1880. Ybor city was noted for having a massive immigrant population and for housing many cigar manufacturing plants, giving it the nickname "Cigar City." Once the Great Depression hit, demand for cigars plummuted and the area was never the same, with residents leaving for greener pastures and many buildings left abandon. However, some residents from so long ago are still there today, in a sense...

Ghost Party Haunted Tours

Ghost Party Haunted Tours offers a variety of ways to experience something paranormal, and is great for families looking to do something spooky. Some of their tours include an overnight lockdown in a haunted building and a paranormal excursion featuring real ghost hunting equipment. You can even dine at a 100 year old restaurant that is itself haunted called the "Carne Chop House."

Ghost Tour of Tampa

Ghost Tour of Tampa offers an evening walk along the streets of Tampa while your guide relates stories of haunted locations and ghosts in the area, while all patrons have to navigate by candlelight.
