What is SpookSource?

SpookSource is the home for a variety of paranormal activities to take part in. You can communicate with other Spookers and share either your real-life hauntings or some scary stories and judge and review the stories submitted by others. You can also prepare for a paranormal excursion of your own with our SpookMap, which displays locations in your area rumored to be paranormal. Make sure you check the Preparations link first! We don't want you getting hurt and joining the haunts!
